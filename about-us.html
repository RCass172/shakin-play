About us

We are a four piece Dublin band who started back in 2012. Having first began playing in local venues we got asked to play a few weddings and from their our passion for events had started.

We play a wide range of music styles to suit your needs just let us know your preference and we will create a setlist tailored to you.

Checkout our upcoming showcase which we generally play once a month or find some previous clips on our YouTube page.

Now offering virtual gigs thanks to COVID so contact us with your questions or queries and we will be more than happy to help create a special day for you.
